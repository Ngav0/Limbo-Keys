# Limbo Keys
A Python recreation of the Geometry Dash Limbo Keys.

# Limbo.exe

If you win it will change your backgroud to "bg.png"
If you lose it will change your background to "lbg.png" and spam 170 images of the Limbo Keys on your desktop

# Limbo Safe.exe

Just the minigame without the background changing or spamming the key pictures on your desktop. Totally safe
